The Best Books According to Lu

My top 3 books of all-time.

红楼梦A Dream of Red Mansions

This book is one of China's four great classics.
It is a family story full of cultural background. The author's writing is very beautiful and
touching.

撒哈拉沙漠Stories of the Sahara

This book was my favorite book in my youth
It tells the story of the writer's personal life in the Sahara Desert, which gave me endless
inspiration and yearning for freedom and travel.

Anna Karenina

This book is written by the Russian writer Leo Tolstoy and is a classic of realistic novels.
This book reflects the living conditions in Russia at that time and the tragic life of Anna,
the main character in the book. The opening sentence, "Happy families are all similar,
but unhappy families are each unhappy in their own way" is the most classic in the book.
